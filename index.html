About Us

shambasalama is a platform...

That deals with the challenge of locusts and quelea birds. Globally, agriculture faces a critical challenge from locust swarms and Quelea bird predation, causing significant crop damage and economic hardships. An innovative Scarecrow System, utilizing advanced technology such as sensors and machine learning, aims to protect agricultural fields from these threats. The study explores the potential of ultrasound-based scarecrow technologies to efficiently and environmentally manage locust and Quelea bird invasions, safeguarding agricultural production and ensuring food security in affected areas.
